Vinegar Oatmeal Cookies

Ingredients

Wet Ingredients

  • 1 Tbsp white vinegar
  • 1/2 Cup melted butter
  • 1/2 Cup brown sugar
  • 1/4 Cup granulated sugar

Dry Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 Tsp baking soda
  • 1 1/2 Cup oatmeal
  • 3/4 Cup flour
  • 1 Pinch salt

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350F
  2. Mix the wet ingredients in a bowl until well blended.
  3. Add in oatmeal, flour, and salt, mix until a soft dough forms. (Dough will be slightly crumbly)
  4. Add baking soda, and knead into dough until completely absorbed.
  5. Make twelve equally-sized balls of dough and place them on a cookie tray and bake for 10 to 15 minutes, or until edges are golden and centers are soft.
